### 8.2.2 通道通信 ✅

- **实现位置**：`tokens.py` TokenType.通道/发送/接收；`ast_nodes.py` ChannelExpr, SendStmt, ReceiveExpr；`concurrency/__init__.py` Channel 类；`interpreter/concurrency.py` exec_send_stmt(), eval_receive_expr()

### 8.2.3 带缓冲的通道 ✅

- **实现位置**：`concurrency/__init__.py` BufferedChannel 类；`interpreter/concurrency.py` eval_channel_expr()

### 8.2.4 选择器（Select） ✅

### 8.3.1 互斥锁 ✅
